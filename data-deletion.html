Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and your right to control your data.

If you have used our service via Facebook Login and wish to have your data deleted from our records, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of all data associated with your account, please send an email to:

info@silverluxegrooming.com

Please use the subject line: **"Data Deletion Request"** and include the email address you use for your Facebook account.

We will process your request and permanently delete all your associated data (including your name, email, and profile preferences) from our database within 72 hours.
