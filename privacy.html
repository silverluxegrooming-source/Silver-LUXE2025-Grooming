Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, make a booking, or contact us. This includes:

  • Contact information (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Booking information (Address, Date, Time)
  • Profile information (Grooming preferences, Favorite Artist, etc.)
  • Payment information (Handled by Paystack)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and provide our services, including:

  • To process your bookings and payments.
  • To personalize your grooming experience.
  • To communicate with you about your appointments, send confirmations, and receipts.
  • To respond to your contact form messages.

3. Information Sharing

[THIS IS A PLACEHOLDER. You must consult a legal professional to draft your official policy.] We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with third-party vendors who help us operate our service (like Supabase and Paystack) but only for the purposes outlined in this policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored in our Supabase database. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.
